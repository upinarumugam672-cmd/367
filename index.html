About IFT-D

Founded in Wolverhampton, England in 2008, IFT-D is a dedicated financial information consulting firm focusing on global financial market research, data analysis and strategic consulting. What began as a small team of 5 financial analysts has grown into an international consultancy with over 70 professionals across 3 continents.

Our journey began during the 2008 financial crisis, when we recognized the critical need for independent, objective financial analysis that could help businesses and investors navigate unprecedented market volatility. Over the past 16 years, we have evolved from a niche market research firm to a full-service financial consultancy, expanding our capabilities while maintaining our core commitment to analytical rigor and client-centric service.
